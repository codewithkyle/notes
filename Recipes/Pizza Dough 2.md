#baking/pizza

3/4 cup of water
1T of sugar
heat to 105 deg
1T of yeast

2 cups of flour
3/4t of salt
1/2t of garlic powder
1/2t of onion powder
1/2t of basil/oregano leaves
mix

add yeast
2T of olive oil
mix until ball
cover with oil
allow dough to rise for 1 hour

preheat oven to 450f

flatten onto pizza stone
add sauce
add cheese
add toppings

cook for 15 minutes
