#baking/buns/potato

3/4 cup of 110deg water
2/3 cup of warm milk
1T yeast
mix

3 cups of bread flour
2/3 cup of potato flour
1t salt
2 eggs
add yeast
Mix until it becomes a ball

Add 4T of butter
Work until butter integrates

Shape into ball
Cover with plastic wrap
wait 2 hours

Make into 8 balls
Cover again
Wait 1 hour

Preheat oven 375
Cover with egg yoke
Cover with seeds
Cook for 18-22 minutes
